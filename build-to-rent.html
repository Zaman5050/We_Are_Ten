our vision

Considered the future of urban living, and recognized as the fastest growing and most noteworthy residential sector in the UK, TEN has played an instrumental role in raising the bar for the interior architecture and design standards of Build-To-Rent schemes.

Our BTR design experience

Having focused on the Build-to-rent sector for the past 5 years, TEN has a deep understanding of the interior design requirements, challenges and objectives for large-scale BTR projects. In addition to this, we are conscious of project budgets and are able to adapt on a project-by-project basis, without compromising on the quality of our designs.

Our consultancy services provide commercial, financial and marketing insights and intelligence to property owners, developers and BTR Operators to assist in decision making. From providing feasibility studies, market analysis or funding services we are able to bring any project to fruition with our network and experience in the sector.

Our unique service offering

We combine our experience in hospitality to deliver tangible and commercially viable, creative solutions to our Build-to-Rent projects. We bring together the latest design, technology and lifestyle trends to our design to ensure that it is operationally sustainable.

Our interior design always looks to bring personality, character and life into urban spaces–creating better living environments that caters to the 21st century resident. We cover the entire lifecycle of the BTR project, and are happy to get involved on new-built projects at its infancy as well as up-and-running properties requiring a refresh. Our highly tailored FF&E Specification and Procurement Service further guarantees that there is little to no difference between 3D models and our on-site execution
