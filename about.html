about us

WHAT WE DO

We work at the intersection of the Residential & Hospitality sectors.

TEN is an international Interior Architecture & Design House specializing in the Residential, Hospitality, and Commercial sectors. Headquartered in London, with additional design houses in Riyadh and Dubai, TEN delivers world-class Interior Design solutions and bespoke FF&E Specification and Procurement services on global projects. Our work emphasizes user experience and sustainability, crafting spaces where people work, live, play, and stay — by design.

What sets us apart from other design houses? We balance the commercial aspirations of our clients with creative design solutions. This is achieved through our in-depth knowledge of the real estate and hospitality industries, their challenges, and how we can solve them through design.

our founders

TEN was founded by Sameer Mahomed and Anya Sokolskaya in 2018. Together, they bring a noteworthy combination of business, creativity and entrepreneurship to the Interior Architecture and Design sector in the UK and the Middle East.

MEET OUR DIRECTORS

Sameer Mahomed & Anya Sokolskaya

As the Co-Founder and Creative Director at TEN, Anya’s work transcends the conventional boundaries of interior design by crafting spaces that harmonize both aesthetic and operational objectives. With over 15 years of experience across the residential, hospitality, and commercial sectors, Anya combined her diversified expertise to focus primarily on the Build-to-Rent (BTR) sector in the past 8 years. Anya succeeds in bringing hospitality quality design to residential projects, and residential aesthetic to hospitality interiors. This commitment to her design ethos and excellence has been instrumental in the success of TEN.

Sameer is a Chartered Accountant and Real Estate Investment Specialist who trained and qualified at Deloitte, working in their Johannesburg, Los Angeles, and London offices for over 9 years. As the Co-Founder and Managing Director at TEN, Sameer spearheads business development in the UK and Middle East with his talent to identify new opportunities and brings his consulting and advisory experience to all of TEN’s c lients. During his time in the Real Estate Hospitality & Leisure Group in London, he served clients such as the Abu Dhabi Investment Authority RE Department, Grosvenor Estates, Marriott, Hard Rock Hotels, Orient Express, NH Hotels, Cushman & Wakefield, Bawadi, Emaar and many more.
