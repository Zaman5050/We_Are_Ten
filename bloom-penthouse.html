Bloom Penthouse

TEN was appointed by Greystar to design the exclusive penthouse at Bloom Nine Elms, situated on the top floor of the recently opened Phase 2 building. The Penthouse has been designed to capture the changing resident landscape of Nine Elms, reflecting the youthful and fun nature of the resident profile.
