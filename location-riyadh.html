OUR DESIGN HOUSE IN SAUDI ARABIA

Bringing our expertise to the Middle East

With a portfolio of projects completed in Riyadh and Jeddah over the past few years, our affiliation with Saudi’s interior architecture and design industry is long-standing. Among others, our clients include leading developers and investors such as Arcadis, Omnium and Qatari Diar. Our growth in the market was our deciding factor to become more accessible to our Saudi Arabian clients with the opening of our offices and on-the-ground team lead by Nour Mansour. With 15 years of experience in the Middle Eastern Architecture and Design sectors, Mansour brings invaluable knowledge on executing projects that resonate with the local audience.

TEN transcends the conventional boundaries of interior design in Riyadh by crafting spaces that harmonize both aesthetic and operational objectives. With the growth of tourism and decerning international travelers visiting Saudi Arabia, TEN brings unique insights of the international standards of design in the hospitality and residential sectors to the table.

OUR DESIGN VISION

The future of design in Saudi Arabia

Designing city living in Saudi Arabia in line with Vision 2030 involves planning for the long-term growth and development of the country as it spearheads design and innovation in both architecture and design. Blending cultural aesthetics and century-old traditions with modern methods of construction and way of life creates an exciting challenge for us as designers experienced in designing for new ways of living & working in the 21st century.

In Saudi Arabia’s city living vision for 2030, the focus is also on human well-being and centricity. Nature, sustainability-inspired designs and community engagement promote mental and physical health, while technology enhances connectivity and convenience, enriching urban living experiences.
