CORE SERVICE

Furniture solutions by design

Our seamless FF&E Procurement Service follows a meticulous 6 phase process. We are involved from concept to installation, as well offering an after-care service which extends well beyond the final handover to our client. Our in-house FF&E service is a natural extension of our design process and offers many advantages–aesthetically, logistically and financially.

Our FF&E Specification and Procurement Service ensures that each project achieves the intended aesthetic when executed on site. By incorporating bespoke furniture, lighting and joinery tailored to the concept and aesthetic of a space, TEN delivers differentiated projects that becomes an extension of your brand.

OUR FF&E PROCESS

Smart Procurement – how and why?

We are experts in curating bespoke furniture packs that is tailored to your project and budget. Smart procurement allows us to select a mix of products from diverse manufacturers and brands, which matches the project aesthetic and fall within your price range. The advantages of manufacturing and procuring in different parts of the world includes:

Reduced risk

There are many risk factors with having all your eggs in one basket. Similarly, we procure furniture pieces from different manufacturers across the world to reduce risks on product delivery.

Improved quality

Our direct lines to manufacturers and our global team allows us to oversee the production of our products and to specify the exact materials which should be used.

Offers product diversity

With the option of manufacturing bespoke furniture designed by TEN, and over 50 trusted suppliers from 13 different countries where we can source FF&E, our range of product diversity is endless.

Improved ESG

Our involvement in the design, manufacturing, shipping and installation of all FF&E products allows us to improve our ESG credentials every step of the way and to be more conscious of the environmental impacts of our creations.

Our ethos

ESG Vision

TEN is committed to all aspects of ESG with regard to its role in both design and furniture procurement. As an important influencer and advisor to both the residential and hospitality industries it is our responsibility to play a leading and active role in responsible and conscious procurement of furniture.

Sustainability

We are experienced in designing and sourcing sustainable products and materials on projects and hold WELL AP certification. Our design ethos places special attention on human well-being and our focus is to make the best use of each space so that the building can be enjoyed by the residents for many years to come.

TEN Second-Life

Second Life offers sustainable solutions to recycling and disposing outdated furniture in the UK. Proudly run by TEN, the program has a network of facilities, charities and organizations who facilitate the distribution of discarded furniture pieces to underprivileged communities in countries such as Africa and Europe.
