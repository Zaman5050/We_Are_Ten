our vision

Whether we're designing large-scale residential developments or private homes, our goal is to deliver a bespoke product that aligns with the brief, draws inspiration from its surroundings, and exceeds the needs of the end user.

The art of designing homes for 21st century living.

TEN’s design practice is constantly evolving as we integrate the latest technological advancements and sustainability innovations into our projects. In the 21st century, residential spaces serve dual purposes, seamlessly transitioning between living, working, staying, and playing. With the rise of work-from-home models and co-living spaces, interior design is increasingly focused on creating versatile public amenity areas within residential developments.

The notion that 'bigger is better' is rapidly giving way to a more mindful approach — designing eco-conscious spaces that meet the needs of users without compromising on sustainability. TEN is a strong advocate of this movement and is proudly WELL certified.

Our bespoke FF&E Service
