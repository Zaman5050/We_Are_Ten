our vision

We approach our commercial projects with the same passion and dedication that define our hospitality and residential work. By incorporating experiential design, we aim to excite, inspire, and transform underutilized spaces within the commercial sector.

The Future of Commercial Design

TEN reinvigorates modern commercial spaces by moving away from cookie-cutter designs and toward environments that tell stories and celebrate individuality.

Our commitment to sustainability and human well-being is at the heart of everything we create. We anticipate a growing trend in merging indoor and outdoor living, incorporating more greenery, natural light, and natural ventilation to craft relaxed, healthy environments in our commercial spaces

A Salute to Hybrid Spaces

Hybrid spaces present an exciting challenge for interior designers, blending elements of the residential, commercial, and hospitality sectors. TEN’s extensive experience across all three gives us a distinct advantage in delivering projects with dual functionalities for diverse users. Our team takes pride in balancing the commercial goals of our clients with innovative design solutions.
