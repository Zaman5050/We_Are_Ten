interior design

CORE SERVICE

Transforming spaces for better living

We are passionate about aesthetically beautiful spaces, but more so, how they have the power to impact our emotions through sight, smell, touch, taste and sound. Applying music and our signature candle scent, Oudh & Rose, as the finishing touches to our projects is an example of our commitment to deliver an experiential product, rather than a decorated space.

TEN takes a holistic approach to interior design that is mindful of its impact on the environment – from applying WELL principles of design to using sustainably sourced products and materials in our FF&E service, we are always striving to improve our ESG credentials.

OUR VISION

Storytelling through spatial design

Our concepts and designs are inspired by the nature of our end user, the context of the physical location of the project and ultimately the story that we would like to tell through the interior architecture and design of the space. TEN also favours the use of direct and indirect biophilic design in our work–creating environments that connect us to nature and improve our mental well-being. Our work has become synonymous with organic forms, earthy tones and the use of plants and natural light to create subtle ques to being in nature.

OUR INTERIOR DESIGN SCOPE

Global experience, local insights, universal design principles.

We are specialists of residential, hospitality and commercial interior architecture and design. Our global team brings 30 years of combined experience to our design houses in London, Riyadh and Dubai. Our in-house team brings creative, operational, and technical expertise to every project, including FF&E sourcing, procurement, visualisation, and Revit integration. Our efficient process saves our clients time and fees by reducing the need for meetings and appointments.

Our ethos

ESG Vision

TEN is committed to all aspects of ESG with regard to its role in both design and furniture procurement. As an important influencer and advisor to both the residential and hospitality industries it is our responsibility to play a leading and active role in responsible and conscious procurement of furniture.

Sustainability

We are experienced in designing and sourcing sustainable products and materials on projects and hold WELL AP certification. Our design ethos places special attention on human well-being and our focus is to make the best use of each space so that the building can be enjoyed by the residents for many years to come.

TEN Second-Life

Second Life offers sustainable solutions to recycling and disposing outdated furniture in the UK. Proudly run by TEN, the program has a network of facilities, charities and organizations who facilitate the distribution of discarded furniture pieces to underprivileged communities in countries such as Africa and Europe.
