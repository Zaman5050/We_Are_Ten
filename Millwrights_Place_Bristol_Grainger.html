Millwrights Place

Nestled in the heart of Bristol, Millwrights Place stands as a testament to the city’s captivating history. This interior design project expertly navigates the delicate balance between old and new, weaving a narrative inspired by Bristol’s legacy of craftsmanship, exploration, and trading. The design concept harmoniously fuses traditional elements with modern sophistication, creating a space that pays homage to the city’s past while embracing contemporary living.

Client:

Grainger PLC

Location:

Bristol, UK

Scope:

Public Space Interior Design, Custom Joinery Design, FF&E Specification & Procurement, Stages 1 - 5, Visualization, Styling and Installation
